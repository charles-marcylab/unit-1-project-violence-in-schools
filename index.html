Cases Peaked in 2021

Fortunatley the worst is behind us, with cases peaking in the beginning of 2021. The decline of the virus correlates proportionaly with the mass increase of vaccinated folk.

The Omicron Variant

The Omicron variant likely will spread more easily than the original SARS-CoV-2 virus and how easily Omicron spreads compared to Delta remains unknown. CDC expects that anyone with Omicron infection can spread the virus to others, even if they are vaccinated or don't have symptoms.

Vaccines Prove To Be Effective

Vaccines to prevent coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) are perhaps the best hope for ending the pandemic. They prevent you from becoming seriously ill, spreading the virus, and contribute to community immunity
