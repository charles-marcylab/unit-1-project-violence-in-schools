Origins and Timeline

Towards the end of 2019, Chinese officials identified an unknown disease called coronavirus. The first death in America occurred in California. It wasn't until March 11, 2020 when the World Health Organization declared the outbreak a pandemic.

Fatality Rates by Ages

Younger people have a much lower fatality rate than those who are older, 40+. This data consists of 2,449 U.S. COVID-19 cases from February 12, 2020, through March 16, 2020.

Common Symptoms

Most people recorded experiencing a fever, dry coughs, fatigue, producing thick mucus, and shortness of breath.
